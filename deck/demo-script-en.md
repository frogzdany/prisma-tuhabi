# Prisma · 5-slide demo script (English)

Total: about 2 minutes 30 seconds. Spoken 1 min 45 s. Live demo 45 s.
Five speakers. Each block tags the slide on screen and who has the clicker.

One-liner (Daniel opens): "Every seller's message routed to its best path, in under thirty seconds."

## LIVE DEMO. Ecatepec, split four ways. 45 s

(In the app. Each speaker takes their beat, points at the screen.)

### Beat 1. Joseph. 10 s

A message lands in WhatsApp. Seller in Ecatepec, wants to move fast.
Here is the UI: single screen, decision always on top, agent trace
on the side.

### Beat 2. Braulio. 10 s

My data layer kicks in. Zone: Ecatepec, high risk tier. Estimated
value: inside the iBuyer range but ruled out by the risk policy.
Every datum typed and validated before the agent touches it.

### Beat 3. Roberto. 13 s

The agent reasons across all of it in under seven seconds. Decision:
no iBuyer because of risk, route to live nurture. And notice this:
the listing cover and the property tour are generated by Prisma too,
with Imagen 4 and Veo 3.

### Beat 4. Denisse. 12 s

Generates the reply with the Sofía voice in Mexican Spanish. Persists
the lead in Supabase. The Pulppo advisor for the zone gets a warm
queue with context, not a cold name. Back to slides.

**Action:** Denisse plays the audio, Cmd + Tab back to Slide 4.
Hand the clicker to Roberto.

## Delivery notes

- One idea per sentence. Slow over fast.
- Every speaker names themselves the first time they take the mic.
- Hand the clicker physically when handing off, with the slide already changed.
- Daniel opens and closes. Anchor presence on stage.
- Joseph kicks the demo, Denisse closes it and returns to slides.
- Slide 2 is the densest: Roberto leads the eyes to the top half (problem)
  and Braulio to the bottom half (resolution). Use your hand if needed.
- If the app fails: Daniel says "we have backup video" and jumps
  straight to Slide 4 via Roberto. Backup video runs 30 s max.
- Daniel closes with "Ready to run" as the anchor line.
